Sign Up & Log In

How Do I Sign Up?

1.     Select “Register” at the top right corner of the Reservasidulu homepage
2.     Select “Register with E-mail”
3.     You will be connected to your Google account
4.     Input your verification code that you have received via-email
5.     Your account has been successfully created

How Do I Log In?

1.     Select the “Log In” button on the top right corner in our website.
2.     Fill in your email address and password that you already create in sign up page.
3.     Click “Log In”.

Password

I Forgot My Password

1.     Select “Log In” on Reservasidulu homepage
2.     Select Forgot Password
3.     Enter the e-mail address you used to register
4.     Enter the verifications code that has been sent to your e-mail
5.     Enter your new password, then click Change Password.

OTP Code

I Didn’t Receive OTP Code

1.     Make sure you are using registered e-mail
2.     If you used the registered e-mail, but still not receive an OTP code, please click Resend

Account Blocked

My Account is Blocked

For security reasons, your account will be blocked after 3 unsuccessful login attempts. Here’s how you can unblock it.

1.     Select “Log In” on Reservasidulu homepage, then choose Forgot Password
2.     Enter your e-mail address
3.     Answer the secret question

Method

What kind of payment methods can I use?

Payment can be made by credit card, Mastercard and Visa. We’re currently unable to accept cash, debit card, or bank transfer.

Transaction Declined

Why has my card transaction been declined?

There are number of reasons why your transaction may have been declined, these could be:

1.     You’ve exceeded your limit
2.     Your card has expired
3.     Invalid credit card number, CVV number, or expiration date.
4.     Different billing address on your payment method.


If you continue to have trouble, we recommend contacting your bank to see if there’s a problem with your card.

Need more help?
