OUR STORY
BEGINS

It’s a blessing when you meet someone that has the same purpose as you. And it’s what makes us dream-driven, passionate, and eager every day. Because we have that someone that will continue to enjoy the things we do every day, and we are blessed to share that purpose together. Not everyone in this world gets to do something like us.

Bruce and Natasha are both veterinarians, working with the most beautiful animals in the world and have been continuing what they’re doing since 2015. They have been in a relationship for 5 years and it's time to tie the knot. So they invite you to join their special day, and celebrate their journey and their passion.
